Case Study - Dual-Mode Real Estate

Aura Estate

Aura Estate is a premium real estate experience built for two distinct high-intent personas: Marcus the investor and Maya the buyer. A single onboarding forks into fully separate modes, each with its own information architecture and decision support tools.

Dual Persona Research

Two users with completely different goals required an architecture that never forces either into a generic flow.

Marcus Sterling — The Investor
A seasoned real estate professional managing high-value assets
Prioritises market data, financial forecasting, and specialised efficiency
Needs: CAP rates, ROI filters, digital document vault, strategic contact
Emotions: Calculating → Focused → Cashflow-led → Decisive
Key feature: one-tap priority offer on top performers
Maya Stephen — The Buyer
A young professional transitioning from renting to owning
Seeking emotional connection to a space; stimulated by complex financial and legal steps
Needs: lifestyle vibe tags, monthly affordability guide, guided agent chat
Emotions: Excited → Overwhelmed → Relaxed → Empowered
Key feature: Sanctuary-first IA and step-by-step readiness guide

Journey Map

Both personas pass through four stages, but what they seek and feel at each point diverges completely.

Discovery and Search

Marcus: filter by data, then verify ROI and legal docs
Maya: browse by lifestyle (Metro, Coffee), find the vibe
Marcus emotion: Calculating → Focused
Maya emotion: Excited → Overwhelmed

Evaluation and Action

Marcus: verify documents, place a high-stakes offer via priority
Maya: understand monthly needs, contact agent with confidence
Marcus emotion: Cashflow-led → Decisive
Maya emotion: Relaxed → Empowered

Proposed Solution

Tailored dual-persona user journey diverging from one common onboarding
Aura Intelligence: activity and lifestyle-based property filtering for Maya
Data-driven analytical dashboards for Marcus’s investment decisions
Direct priority agent contact for Marcus’s high-intent offer moments
Integrated financial clarity tools for confident shortlisting

Information Architecture

Common onboarding with a persona toggle leads to two entirely separate navigation structures.

Investor Mode

Dashboard: overview and top-performing assets
Insights: analysis, opportunities, market heatmaps
Market: 12-month forecasts and Aura Intelligence updates
Value: risk analysis and Pro-forma calculations
Profile: portfolio strategy and digital vault

Buyer Mode

Dashboard: sanctuary search with lifestyle vibe tags
Favourites: vision board and compare grid
Guide: home readiness journey with step milestones
Inbox: agent conversations and AI helper
Profile: digital sanctuary and support documents
