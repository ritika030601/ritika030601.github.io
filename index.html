About Me

My journey started in the terminal, shipping app features as a software engineer.

I realized that great products need more than clean code. Users need clear, intuitive experiences that make them feel understood. That insight is what moved me from app engineering into UI/UX design.

Today I use my engineering rigor to inform every design decision. I understand how data flows, how components scale at the system level, and how to communicate precisely with developers to ensure the final product looks and feels exactly as intended. I don't just design for the screen — I design for the system.

I do not stop at handoff. I stay involved through PR review to ensure implementation matches design intent.

Design Philosophy

Uncover the why
before solving the what.

A structured, human-centered approach that I apply consistently — from the first research call to the final QA pass.

01
Empathize
Listen
Deep dive into user needs through interviews, observation, and data analysis to find the real why behind behavior — not just surface-level pain points.
02
Ideate
Shape
Synthesize complex data into clear, actionable design strategies. Prioritize ruthlessly. Align with engineering constraints from the start — no impractical handoffs.
03
Prototype
Build
Craft high-fidelity prototypes that feel real enough to test. Use my engineering background to specify interactions at the component level — precise, not vague.
04
Test
Refine
Test with real users, measure against success metrics, and iterate until the experience is effortless. Shipping is not the end — it is a data point.
